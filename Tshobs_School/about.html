At STIS Sir Tshobs International School, we believe that education is a journey, not a destination. Our dedicated team of teachers and staff are passionate about creating a supportive and inclusive learning enviroment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and a love of learning.

Admission and Programs

Empowering Your Academic Journey

Tuition Fee
We strive to make quality education accessible and affordable for all. Our tuition fee is $50 per month, making us an excellent value for families seeking a world-class education.
This affordable fee includes:
  1. Access to state-of-the-art facilities and resources
  2. Expert instruction from experience teachers
  3. A wide range of extracurricular activities and programs
  4. Support services, such as counseling and academic advising

We believe that every child deserves an exceptional education, regardless of their financial background. Our affordable tuition fee helps make that vision a reality.
Application Form
Ready to be part of our community? To apply, please visit our school in person to collect and submit an application form.
A one-time registration fee of $5 is required to process your application. This fee is non-refundable and non-transferable.
  1. Completed application form
  2. Birth certificate
  3. Passport-sized photo
  4. Academic records(if applicable)

Our admissions team will review your application and contact you to discuss next steps.
O'level
Our O'level program is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in core subjects, preparing them for success in their future academic pursuits. Our experienced teachers deliver engaging and interactive lessons, helping students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. We offer a range of subjects, including: Mathematics, English Language, Science, Humanities and Foreign Languages.
A'level
Our A'level program is tailored to meet the needs of students who are preparing for university or other post-secondary education. We offer a range of subjects, allowing students to specialize in areas of interest and passion. Our experienced teachers provide expert guidance and support, helping students develop advanced critical thinking, research, and analyticsal skills. We offer subjects in: Commercials(Accounts, Geography, Business studies, Pure Mathematics) and Arts(Literature in English, F.R.S, Shona Language, Heritage).

Quality Education

At S.T.I.S (Sir Tshobs International School), we offer a high-quality education that prepares our students for future success. Our experienced teachers deliver a rigorous curriculum that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity.

Safe and Supportive Enviroment

We provide a safe, supportive, and inclusive enviroment that allows our students to feel comfortable, confident, and valued. Our school is commited to preventing bullying, promoting social-emotional learning, and fostering positive relationships between students, teachers, and staff.

Personalized Attention and Support

At S.T.I.S(Sir Tshobs International School), we recognize that every child learns differently and has unique needs and strengths. Thats why we offer personalized attention and support to each student, including small class sizes, individualized learning plans, and access to counseling and academic support services.

Highly Qualified Teachers

Our school boasts a team of highly qualified teachers who are passionate about education and dedicated to helping our students achieve their full potential. Our teahers are certified, experienced, and committed to ongoing professional development.

Character Development and Values-Based Education

At Sir Tshobs International School, we place a strong emphasis on character development and values-based education. We teach our students important life skills, such as emphathy, kidness, and responsibility, helping them become well-rounded, compassionate, and socially responsible individuals.
